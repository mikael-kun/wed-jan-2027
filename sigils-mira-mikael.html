Sigils for the six contacts — one seal for each star in the reading

Each seal carries one contact from the reading — drawn from its meaning, never its raw glyph, so it reads as a mark and renders anywhere. Six contacts, six seals.

I

The Recognition · Tunnistus 再会

3°–4° Sagittarius

The Moon's crescent and the Sun meet at the same degree — Uranus's spark between them, Pluto's depth beneath. Night met the light and knew it.

II

The Threshold · Kynnys 縁

6° Libra

Her path ends at a door that is him. The star stands on the threshold; the way has already arrived.

III

The Current · Virta 風

1°–3° Aquarius

Two Aquarian air-currents, one lightning-bolt piercing them both. Thought and thrill on a single wire.

IV

The Love Remembered · Muistettu rakkaus 面影

22°–26° Capricorn

The beloved above the water's surface, their image beneath it. A dream laid over a memory.

V

The Star-Origin · Tähtisynty 銀河

26°34′ Sagittarius

Neptune on the Galactic Centre: the one door that opens onto the turning heart of the galaxy.

VI

The Covenant · Liitto 契り

13° Sagittarius

Two rings joined by a single star, set on a line of granite. A joy built to last.

Use · how they're meant to live

They're a set, not six logos. The first — The Recognition — is the same family as your marriage mark, so it can stand as the primary seal; the others work as section marks down the reading, as a chapter row, or singly on a card, a stamp, an end-page. One colour each, so they cut to foil, wax, or a single-ink print without changing.
